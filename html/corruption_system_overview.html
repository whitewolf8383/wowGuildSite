CORRUPTION SYSTEM

Corrpution is a new system mechanic designed to replace the old Titanforged system. Introduced in patch 8.3, corruption effects provide players with special procs that can either deal direct damage or increase specific stats, at the cost of corruption. Most corruptions have 3 tiers of effect that increase with the increase of corruption cost. These corrputions can be found on many armor and weapon items and can now be purchased from the vendor MOTHER located in the hearts chamber.

Corruption Cost: 8

Tier 2: Increases Avoidance by 12% of your haste rating
