BREWMASTER

Brewmaster is the tanking specialization of Monks, and they possess a wide array unique abilities. Brewmasters do not overly rely on avoiding damage through dodging and parrying attacks. Instead, they have Staggering damage, which causes only a portion of damage taken to be done to the Monk instantly. The rest of the damage is done over the course of 10 seconds (with the possibility for the Monk to remove part of the non-instant damage by using Purifying Brew).

WARLOCK

Warlocks are the summons of demons and hell fire. While being a vrey fun class to play, it does take a considerable amount of rotation control to establish a high level of dps. What makes this class so valuable though is their extreme ability to stay alive with movement and self healing abilities and having demons that can tank, silence, and even heal. Add that warlocks also supply players with an additional movement ability, "lock rocks" to self heal, and the ability to resurrect, one can see that they become a well know staple in both raids and mythic plus.

AFFLICTION

Affliction Warlocks excel at sustaining high single-target and multiple-target damage. Most of your damage comes from the application of harmful damage over time effects (DoTs). This allows them to effectively cleave on targets that are spread around an area, something many specializations struggle with or are flat out unable to do. Just like the other Warlock specializations, Affliction Warlocks bring the Demonic Gateway ability to the raid, which can be an invaluable mobility cooldown.
