Shadowlands Zones

Once a soul leaves the world of the living, they must journey here, Oribos, an ancient city built in the middle of Shadowlands. This is where all souls will be judged and sent to their respective afterlives. As adventurers, this too will be your entrence into the Shadowlands. Yet, because you are not dead, you will be able to journey through all the realms as you complete quests and fight your way to level 60.

Ardenweald

A mysterious and enchanted forest, ruled by the Night Fae, Ardenweald is home to souls who dedicated their lives to nature and whos job it is to collect anima, infusing their lives with spirits and old knowledge to prepair for reentry into the world of the living.

Creature Types

Faeries

Faeries are the tricksters of the forest who use song to soothe away pain and lure lesser creatures in to be captured and ground into poultices. Use caution as they are easily offended.

Tirnenn

As the eldests of the Fae court, these generally friendly creatures are calm and patient, but dangerous in battle and capable of terraforming the land around them in an instant

Bastion

A vast land of gleaming spires and sweeping vistas, ruled by the Kyrians, Bastion is home to those who spent their life in service to others. It's pristine crystalline skies are welcoming to all who see it and the Kyrians use this land to usher in the souls from the land of the living.

Creature Types

Cloudrunner

Cloudrunners are mighty unicorns whos speed and endurance can't be matched. They are capable of shooting bursts of anima from their horns.

Etherwyrm

Etherwyrms are wind serpent-like creatures who are drawn to streams od anima for feeding.

Larion

Lion-like creatures, larions are very territorial and will attack anyone that comes onto their property. They attack with large claws and teeth.

Vulpin

Vulpin are fox-like creatures that are capable of employing stealth, reinforcing their fur, and increaseing speed when using anima.

Maldraxxus

A war-torn land, governed by the necrolords and home to the necromancers and abomination. The is the place where souls fight to see who is the stronest, raging against the enemies of death to see who will become the champions and gain great power, who will be torn down and turned in fuel to create weapons.

Creature Types

Gladiators

These champions of the House of the Chosen are tireless warriors, always training with their runic weapons and looking for their next battle.

Plague House Remnant

Mindless animated creatures, these failed experiments roam the grounds of Maldraxxus looking to feed on poor souls who think they have found a safe place to rest.

Soul-Rotted Flesh

Monolithic guardians of Maldraxxus, these Amalgamations of fallen soldiers patrol the grounds of Maldraxxus looking for enemies to crush.

Aranakk

The defeated assassins and scouts of the House of Eyes, these nomads retains many secrets, ones they are willing to share, for a price.

Revendreth

In this land of gothic inspired spires and dark secrets, the vampiric Venthyr are the formost experts in the torture and discipline of souls. Afterall, this is the land where those who only served themselves are sent to be redeemed. Though vicious in their work, the Venthyr also seek the pleasures and comforts of high society.

Creature Types

Stone Legion

Carved by Stonewrights, these creations are infused with anima and forced to serve their masters. Although, mosthold a sense of infallible loyalty and will fight for their masters.

Dredger

These servents of Venthyr, born of the primordial muck, hold a can-do, will-do mentality and a soul focus of appeasing their masters, even if that means scrificing themselves for entertainment.

Stoneborn

As members of the Venthyr's army, these gargoyes fight of the enemies of the Venthyr using martial weapons and enhanced mobility.

Gargons

These monstrosities are stone creatures, carved into terrifing creatures and prowl the streets of Revendreth in order to maintain peace through fear or force.

The Maw

Home to the most vile and irredeemable souls, this land is ruled by the jailer. All souls deemed to be a threat to Shadowlands by the Arbiter are cast into this nightmareish hole. However, with the machine of death broken, all souls are now being poured directly into this hell.

Creature Types

Mawsworn

The minions of the jailer, mawsworn, are enemies to all who step foot into the Maw. Trained to bring pain and suffering to the most wicked souls.

death's Essence

Soul energy trapped within the Maw for untold ages coalesce into malevolent manifestations that wander the dark reaches of the Maw.

Charred Behemoth

These brutish iron-clad creatures crave carnage and use their brawn to pulverize everything that stands in their way.

Soul Eater

Malicious beasts that lurk the Maw, these savage terrors rend their prey with wicked talons, relishing the screams of their victims before consuming them soul and all.
