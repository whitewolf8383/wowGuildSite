Shadowlands Overview

New Character Startups

For as long as Warcraft has been around, players have had starting areas directly related to the character type they choose. That means Night Elves start at Shadowglen, Humans start at Northshire Abbey and so on. With the coming of shadowlands, all new players will start in a new zone where they will level up to 10 before choosing an expansion to continue with. This area will give plenty of quests and will have new players kill a raid style boss before exiting the new starting area.

New Playing Zones

With the destruction of thw vale that seperated Shadowlands from Azeroth comes an all new adventure. Shadowlands, the home to the souls that have passed from the world of living, offers 5 new zones for adventures to quest through along with 8 new dungeons, 4 leveling and 4 max level dungeons, as well as a new, ever changing dungeon that players can solo or group run through. This dungeon offers unique rewards that players will need to collect to craft legendary weapons and gear.

New Covenant System

With all these new areas for adventurers to explore, it only makes since that they should find freinds among the numberous enemies that wish to see their demise. In shadowlands, players will be introduced to four new groups, each with a convenant that players will get to choose to gain unique rewards and playing abilities, but choose wisely as changing covenants may be easy, but returning to an abandon one may end your warfighting career.
