Shadowland Covenants

Through your journey in Shadowlands, you have encountered many creatures, both friend and foe. You have fought your way to the ranks of elders and now have the chance to choose your covenient and reap the great rewards thereof. But, with all these possibilities, you can only choose one, the one that will empower you to become even greater.

Kyrian

As the rulers over Bastion, these angelic creatures are the guides that shuttle souls between the land of the living and the land of the dead. The Kyrian value humility, virtue, righteousness, and service. Joining their covenant wil allow ou to call a steward who will provide you a Phial of Serenity, restoring health and removing all curse, disease, poison, and bleed effects.

Summon Steward Call your steward to bring you a Phial of Serenity that can be consumed to restore 15% of your health and remove all Curse, Disease, Poison, and Bleed Effects. Your Steward additionally offers access to a selection of useful amenities, each once per day.
Instant Cast, 5 min cooldown. Lasts 3 min.

Necrolord

Rulers of Maldraxxus and military guardians of Shadowlands itself, these ruthless warlords favor strength and power over all else. Should you choose to become one of their ranks, you will have to proof yourself always as the Necrolords only believe in survival of the fittest. Should you prevail, you will gain the ability of fleshcraft, capable to shielding yourself with flesh and bone and sheildign yourself for 20% of your health. Should you stand on your fallen enemy, you will improve your sheild to 50% of your health.

Fleshcraft Form a shield of flesh and bone over 4 sec that prevents damage equal to 20% of your maximum health. Channeling near a corpse claims their essence to grow the shield, up to 50% of your maximum health. This is most effective against powerful enemies.
10 yd range, 4 sec channel, 2 min cooldown.

Night Fae

The peaceful and humble rulers of the Ardenweald live to nurture souls in hope that one day, those souls would be sent back to the world of the living for rebirth. Their endless work for the Kyrain and other powerful souls in Shadowlands play a critical rule in the flow of anima. Many of those with strong ties to nature seek out the Night Fae and should you join their convenant, you would gain the soulshape ability, shapeshifting and increasing your speed by 30% and even teleport should you reactivate soulshape.

Soulshape Turn into a Vulpin, increasing movement speed by 30%. You may reactivate Soulshape to teleport 10 yds forward. Lasts 12 sec, or indefinitely while in a rest area.
Instant Cast, 1.5 min cooldown.

Venthyr

The vampiric conven of Revendreth are hosts of the damned. They are the punishers of all those souls who sought out wicked ways while in the world of the living. Souls that are sent here seek redemption from their past to either becoming punishers themself or transend to another convenant. Should you join the ranks of the punishers, you too will be able to wind through the shadows using Door of shadow to attack your enemies or defend your allies.

Door of Shadows Wend through the shadows, appearing at the targeted location.
35 yd range, 1.5 sec cast, 1 Charge, 1 min recharge
