WHO ARE WE

Doodles was formed as a safe place for playes to learn raids and mythic+ dungeons. As a teaching guild, we bring in new members of all race/class specializations and provide them guidance in dungeons and raids. Our goal is to build a strong central core of officers to provide information for every spec and build a solid guild full of fun loving, energetic people.
