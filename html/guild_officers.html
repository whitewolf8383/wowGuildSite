GUILD OFFICERS

Our guild officers are the heart and soul of the guild. We are passionate about playing and helping others. We perform the legwork to provide all our guild members a fun, safe place to learn their characters. We create fun events, schedule group runs through dungeons and raids, and simply enjoy the time we have together. We are always looking for people with the same passion to fill officer roles. If you are part of our guild, let one of us know your interested.
